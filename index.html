"About us"

"The International Society for Universal Dialogue is a philosophical society dedicated to promoting international and intercultural dialogue, founded in Warsaw, Poland in 1989."

"Why We Exist"

The main purpose of the society is to pursue, by means of scholarly work, the gradual realization of a decent, peaceful, and just world order. Towards this goal, ISUD produces biannual international conferences to facilitate an ethos of universal dialogue. ISUD is committed to promoting philosophical discourse intended to encourage the emergence of a more decent and humane world order by promoting dialogue to actualize the highest and richest human values in all dimensions of life.
